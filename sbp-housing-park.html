INSIGHTS INTO NH-22,Dera Bassi

A Jewel in the Tricity Crown

Dera Bassi situated on Chandigarh - Delhi highway, has transformed into a major satellite town of Chandigarh. Dera Bassi has witnessed enormous growth and has developed as residential, industrial and educational hub of the region.

With development of nature parks coming up at 70 acres across Dera Bassi, life full of nature and calmness is awaiting like never before. Several top notch educational institutes such as FDDI, Thapar School of Management and Sri Sukhmani Group etc have come up in the vicinity.

And just a 3 minutes signal free drive opens doors to unlimited recreation and shopping activities at Haldiram's, Mc Donald's, KFC, WalMart, Metro, Decathlon and Paras Downtown mall.

With coming up of 200 ft (PR 7 road) and new international airport just around the corner, Dera Bassi is where all the future real estate action is going to be.
