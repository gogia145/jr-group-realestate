About Us

About Us

JR GROUP is a privately owned and run South Australian based GROUP delivering unmatched residential investments and services for the Australian property market. Quality and Return on investment (ROI) for our clients is our primary focus which continues to exceed the already high standard of our past and present projects. With our two main divisions JR GROUP and JR REAL ESTATE ensuring projects are constructed and sold/leased respectively with the maximum forecast growth, clients can ensure that our strict quality control and investment methods cannot be compromised. Experience, Integrity & Success JR GROUP possesses over a decade of solid experience developing residential and commercial projects. He also boasts a track record of generating healthy return for investors. JR Group is solely focused on Australia’s most valuable real estate market:ADELAIDE. Our expertise encompasses all facets of property development, including sourcing prime sites, project management, architectural design, council approval, sales and marketing. JR Group’s success is founded on the strength of our relationships. From key consultants to local governments, we have forged trusting, durable partnerships with a large number of investors. JR Group is closely aligned with a reliable construction company—this greatly minimises the construction risks often associated with property development.
