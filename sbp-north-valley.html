INSIGHTS INTO Sec-127,Greater Mohali

Sector 127 falls in Mohali district in the state of Punjab and is at a close distance from Chandigarh. Sector 127 facilitates mainly road and rail transport with NH-21 passing through this region and railway station in a substantially short distance. The locale is flourished with educational institutes of engineering, polytechnic and even bar-tending. Market and malls are in abundance as well for daily necessities. Sector 127 has developed immensely under the eye of GMADA. From a real estate point of view, the locale now serves as a good area to reside due to its access to all the required amenities in a short distance. Presence of reputed educational institutes and abundant housing opportunities have rightfully qualified Sector 127 as a Real Estate favorite.
